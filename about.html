WeeUse is...

a PLATFORM dedicated to promoting the circular economy for preloved baby and kids’ stuff.

a COMMUNITY of parents who are conscious of our choices and the impact they have on people and the planet, and believe that baby and kid items should be given more than the wee bit of use that they often get before ending up in a landfill.

a MARKETPLACE where parents can earn money by selling and lending their outgrown or unused baby and kid items, while providing other families a cost-saving alternative to buying new.

Community Guidelines

As a WeeUse community member, you agree to...

1. Be kind, respectful, and considerate. By joining WeeUse, you're agreeing to be a part of a community of people who care about and respect one another. Please do not spam or treat WeeUse as a platform for advertising, being offensive, or harassing others.

2. Be honest and transparent. Sellers and lenders should describe any defects your items may have and be honest about the condition of the item.

3. Clean your items before selling or loaning them out. No one wants to receive something dirty. Please consider if you would be happy receiving the item you are selling/loaning out for your own child to use.

4. Keep it within WeeUse. Although you may be tempted to take communications and transactions off-site to avoid service fees, this isn’t good for our community and can lead to awkwardness and disputes between members. Buyers/sellers who are regularly taking transactions off-site may be banned from using WeeUse.

5. Be a good communicator. selling or loaning them out. Please check your messages regularly to ensure buyer/borrower requests, questions, or concerns are answered promptly.
