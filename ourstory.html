Our Story

It wasn’t long after my daughter Cece was born in 2019 I realized just how much stuff babies need and use, and also how quickly they outgrow things. Buying everything new for my baby was going to be out of the question, as it’d be too expensive and wasteful. So I began searching for alternative ways to get necessities for my baby - trawling online marketplaces, buying and borrowing items from friends with older children, and visiting thrift stores with fingers crossed they had what I was looking for.

While these methods have sometimes been successful, I’ve kept thinking that there must be a more efficient way to find preloved things for my kids. A way that didn’t require spending hours searching online marketplaces for reliable sellers located in my part of town, being disappointed by unanswered bids, or sifting through mountains of baby clothes at the thrift store in hopes of finding the right size.

We live in downtown Toronto, surrounded by families with kids. How nice it’d be to have a place where other families could easily list their baby items for sale and I could just as easily find exactly what I needed nearby.

A place where not only could I buy things, I could also borrow seasonal items, gear, clothes, and toys for just as long as needed and return them when we were done using them.

A place for me to find other families who want to give our once treasured and now unused things another life. Or for me to make space in my home by lending out items that Cece has outgrown but I would like to have for my toddler, Jojo, to use later.

A place rooted in kindness and respect.

This place didn’t exist, so I decided to create it. I hope you will find it useful too :)

Xing Brew
Mom of Cece and Jojo / Founder of WeeUse
