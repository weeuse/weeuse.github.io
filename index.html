Why WeeUse?

Because babies and kids grow so quickly and most of the things that they need or want will only be used for a wee bit of time. We want to provide families with an easy way to save space, money, and the environment by buying and borrowing kid and baby gear from people in your local community.

Sell

Selling your preloved baby and kid items on WeeUse is simple and stress-free! Find friendly buyers and securely accept payment for clothing, toys, gear, and other items that you no longer need so that you can make room for the next phase!

Lend

Perhaps you have a crib your toddler has outgrown and you want to save for your next baby. Or maybe you have an extra stroller collecting dust in the closet. Safely lend out your items to make space and money while giving others the chance to enjoy them for short periods of time.

Give

Have you been on the receiving end of lots of gifts and hand-me-downs and want to pass them along? Just feeling generous? Give away your outgrown and unused items to other families who will give them another life.

Our Story

Meet Xing, mom of Cece and Jojo. Unsatisfied with the existing options for acquiring preloved baby items for her kids in their home city of Toronto, Xing decided to create a new marketplace where she and other parents can easily buy and borrow baby and kid items from families nearby, and just as easily pass along and share things they no longer need. And not just a regular online marketplace, but a community rooted in kindness and respect.

Travel Light
