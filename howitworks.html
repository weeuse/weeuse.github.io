How it works

Buying, Selling, lending, and borrowing on WeeUse is easy and secure! Don't hesitate to contact us if you have questions or need additional support.

1. Create a free account

2. Create a listing

Choose "For sale"” to start making your listing.

Be as descriptive and thorough as possible! The more detailed your listing is, the more interest and requests it will generate. Please also be transparent about any defects your item may have.

We suggest uploading a few photos, as this will help your item stand out. You can add a stock image too, but it’s best to have at least one photo of your actual item for sale.

If you are willing to deliver/ship your item, mark that option in your post, along with the delivery fee. If you are only able to deliver to certain neighbourhoods, please mention that in the description.

3. How transactions happen

If someone is interested, they will send you a message and/or request to buy your item, which you accept or decline. If you are happy to sell the item to the interested buyer, accept the request so that the payment process can be initiated.

4. What happens after an item sells

If you accept a sale, the money leaves the account of the buyer and goes to Stripe (our payment processing system). Stripe then connects the buyer and the seller's accounts, and sends the money to you. For the first time around this can take up to 10 working days. After your first sale, you will be paid faster.

Once you accept the sale request, you can go ahead and have the item collected or delivered because this means that the money is already with Stripe and on its way to you. Communicate with the buyer via the WeeUse messaging system to set up a time for pick-up, get a delivery address, etc.

5. Prepare your item

Please clean the item, pack it with care, and if you like, add a nice message to your buyer. All of us parents can do with a bit of extra love :)

Lending out your unused things is a great way to save space (and make some money!) while giving others the chance to enjoy them for short periods of time.

1. Create a free account

2. Create a listing

Choose “For loan” to start making your listing.

You will be able to set a price per 'day', 'week', or 'month'. Select the unit based on the minimum time you're willing to loan the item out. For instance, if you don't want to loan an item for any less than a week at a time, select 'week' as the pricing unit.

If you'd like to offer a different rate for different borrowing periods (e.g., $10/week or $20/month), you can create two listings for the same item with one priced per week and one priced per month.

Add a detailed description and a few nice photos. You should also indicate a minimum/maximum borrowing period in your listing description, as well as dates your item is unavailable.

3. Protecting your items

If you’re worried that something might happen to a valuable item, you can easily purchase insurance for as little as $5 from our partner company that will cover damage or theft.

You also have the option to request that the borrower signs a Loan Agreement, send you a copy of their photo ID for verification, and/or if a deposit is needed. Please indicate this in the listing description.

4. How transactions happen

If someone is interested, they will send you a message and/or request to borrow your item, which you accept or decline. If your item is available during the time they need and you are happy to loan the item out to the interested individual, accept the request so that the payment process can be initiated.

4. What happens after an item is borrowed

If you accept a loan, the money leaves the account of the borrower and goes to Stripe (our payment processing system). Once the transaction has been confirmed, they money will be sent to you. For the first time around this can take up to 10 working days. After your first transaction, you will be paid faster.

Once you accept the request, you can go ahead and have the item collected or delivered because this means that the money is already with Stripe and on its way to you. Communicate with the borrower via the WeeUse messaging system to confirm borrow dates, terms, set up a time for pick-up, get a delivery address, etc.

5. Prepare your item

Please clean the item, pack it with care, and if you like, add a nice message to the borrower. All of us parents can do with a bit of extra love :)

6. Return of items

The borrower should return your item on or before the end of the borrowing period. You are welcome to send a gentle reminder a few days prior to the deadline to confirm details on where and when the item should be returned.

You can create an account and add as many listings for sale or lend as you’d like for FREE.

Service fees are only charged once your item has been sold or loaned out, and are reinvested back into maintaining and improving this platform and community.

6% of each sale will go to our platform so we can keep running our service and developing new features and 2.9% + $0.30 will cover Stripe payment processing fees.

As an example, if you list and sell an item for $50.00:

$3.00 will go to maintaining the WeeUse platform

$1.75 will be cover Stripe’s payment processing

$45.25 will go to you!

You can request to borrow the item or send the lender a message in the WeeUse messaging system to ask about availability and anything else you'd like to know about the item.

The item's owner will approve or decline your request. While you wait, you can communicate with the item owner via the messaging system or give them a little nudge if they haven’t responded after a few days. Lender's may request a photo ID or signing a Loan Agreement for more valuable items.

5. What happens next

Once the owner approves, you will receive a confirmation email. Communicate with the lender to arrange pick-up/delivery and other logistics. Don't hesitate to communicate with them during the borrowing period if you have any questions. If you are finished with the item sooner than your borrowing period, you can let the owner know and arrange to have it returned early.

6. Returning and item

Please clean the item and make sure it's packed and ready to be returned by the end of your borrowing period.

7. Leave a review

Please let the owner know how the experience was for you. Reviews are an important part of the WeeUse community as it helps lenders know if they’re doing a great job or need to improve. It also helps WeeUse ensure satisfaction of our members.
