FAQ

Don't hesitate to contact us if you have questions that are not answered on this page.

If you accept a sale, that means the money leaves the account of the buyer and goes to Stripe (the system we use for processing payments) and is on its way to you (which can take up to 10 days for your first sale), so there is no need to wait before having the item collected or delivered.

We use Stripe to ensure all transactions are as secure as possible. Stripe is one of the top, secure systems for online payment in the world. Sometimes there are delays in the transfer, but you should receive your payment within 10 working days. We get notified if there is an issue with Stripe, and if so, we will let you know. Do contact us if you have any questions and we will do our best to help you out.

You can create an account and add as many listings for sale or lend as you’d like for FREE. Service fees are only charged once your item has been sold or is loaned out, and are reinvested back into maintaining and improving this platform and community. 7.1% of each sale will go to our platform so we can keep running our service and developing new features and 2.9% will cover Stripe payment processing fees.

Our platform does not support cash in hand on collection. A few reasons: I have personally sold items on other online marketplaces, agreed on a price with a buyer, only to have them try to negotiate a lower price or “not bring enough cash” upon pickup, which put me in an awkward spot. I have also purchased items where I’ve been required to send a large e-transfer with fingers crossed the seller would follow through. If you sell through us, you receive the payment straight to your bank account.

WeeUse uses Stripe to keep all your banking details secure and we don’t have access to your bank details - it sits on Stripe's super secure platform.

WeeUse also relies on service fees collected through transactions on our site to maintain and grow our platform. If several community members take their transactions off the site, we wouldn’t be able to improve our services (or continue to exist!)

WeeUse is a peer-to-peer marketplace - i.e., an online platform that connects buyers and sellers, lenders and borrowers. We are here to to provide a space for you to buy and sell / lend and borrow, and to support you and try to help sort things out in case of issues. However, we are not responsible for items that are bought, sold, loaned, borrowed, or given away for free through the website.

Any transaction made through WeeUse is between the seller and buyer / lender and borrower. If you have any questions or concerns about the things you're buying/borrowing, would like more information or photos, or have complaints about an item you received, we advise messaging and trying to resolve the issue with the owner.
