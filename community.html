Grange Park Families Meet Up & Kids Toys and Books Swap

Sunday, August 21, 2022 | 9:30-11:30am

Come out to meet other families with kids from the Grange Park community!

You are also welcome to bring toys and books that your kid(s) no longer use to exchange with others. Hopefully there will be items for all age groups and you'll find something new and fun for your child!

We'll have a table set up near the splash pad with some snacks for everyone to enjoy.

Any books/toys that aren't claimed by the end of the meetup around 11:30am will be listed for free on WeeUse.

Please feel free to invite your friends!

RSVP HERE

A few notes on the toy/book swap:
1) The general rule is that you can take up to the numbers items as you bring (e.g., if you bring 3 books, you can take up to 3 books/toys), though if you bring something fancy/of high value, you can swap it for a few smaller things. We can all use our best judgement.
2) Please don't bring any toys that are broken.
3) Please make sure your items are cleaned before bringing them to the swap.

In case this event has to be cancelled due to bad weather, we'll post an update on the facebook event page. Hope to see you all there!
